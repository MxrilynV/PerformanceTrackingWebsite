Performance Tracking App

Team Members: Marilyn Vazquez, Ian Brewer, Eron Yeung, & Jesse Avendano

Project Description

With the rise in popularity of rowing machines due to their numerous health benefits, it is important to
address the risks of injury associated with rowing exercises. Through our research, we found that existing
gym apps mostly offer pre-set luxuries, such as video training, meal plans, and workout tracking, but do
not provide real-time personal statistics for weight lifting or ensure the safety of the user. While we
discovered a similar approach to our design in other implementations such as, flex, it does not prioritize
user safety. This led us to conclude that rowers should be able to safely row without the worry of injury
and have their performance tracked to better understand their progress and have the correct amount of training.
To achieve our objectives, my team and I are designing an application and system that fully encompasses the
needs of rowers and ensures their safety. Our project involves calculating the weight, power, distance of the
bar traveled, and velocity, while also ensuring the user is using the equipment safely. Our first step in the
design phase was finding a standard rowing machine that is commonly used through many gyms. Which led us to the
Concept2 Model D Indoor Rowing Machine, that was provided by San Francisco Stateâ€™s Athletic Department. Looking
at the mechanical components of the Concept 2 Rowing Machine and understanding the way it functions, we were able
to identified the necessary sensors and programming needed to collect and display the desired statistics in real-time

Project Goal

The objective of this project is to enhance gym-goers' workout experience by enabling them to monitor their
performance in real-time while using exercise equipment. To achieve this, we propose to develop an additional
device that works in tandem with equipment such as lifting and rowing machines, providing users with immediate
performance feedback. The collection of data from exercise equipment has been shown to have numerous benefits,
including tracking performance over time, setting achievable goals, promoting self-motivation, ensuring proper
equipment use for safety, and improving overall experience.
